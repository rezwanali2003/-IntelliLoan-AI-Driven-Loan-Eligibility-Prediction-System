How it Works

Our loan eligibility predictor uses a combination of factors to determine if you qualify for a loan:

  • Monthly Income
  • Monthly Expenses
  • Credit Score
  • Employment Status
  • Value of Collateral
  • Loan Amount
  • Loan Term
  • Interest Rate
  • Down Payment

Once you enter these details into our calculator, it will analyze the information and provide you with a loan eligibility prediction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Anyone who is interested in getting a loan can use our eligibility calculator to determine if they qualify.
What factors are used to calculate loan eligibility?
The calculator takes into account several factors, including your monthly income, expenses, credit score, employment status, value of collateral, loan amount, loan term, interest rate, and down payment.
